# 11+ Galaxy 🌌

A space-themed 11+ assessment platform where children explore galaxies, visit planets, and complete missions to master exam topics.

## Concept
- Each subject is a galaxy, each topic is a planet
- Children customise their Space Explorer avatar
- Gamified progression with XP, ranks, and achievements
- Personalised insights and recommendations
- Parent portal for progress tracking and billing
- Admin portal for content and payment management
